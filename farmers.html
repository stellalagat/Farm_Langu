Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about joining Farm Langu

How much does it cost to join Farm Langu?

Joining Farm Langu is completely free! We offer a free Starter plan that allows you to list up to 5 products and start selling immediately. As your business grows, you can upgrade to our Professional or Enterprise plans for additional features and support.

How do I receive payments from customers?

We support multiple payment methods including M-Pesa, Airtel Money, bank transfers, and mobile banking. Payments are processed automatically and sent directly to your registered mobile money account or bank account within 24 hours of successful delivery.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes! We have partnerships with reliable logistics companies across Kenya to handle pickup from your farm and delivery to customers. You just need to prepare your products according to our packaging guidelines, and we'll handle the rest.

What types of products can I sell?

You can sell fresh produce (fruits, vegetables, herbs), dairy products, eggs, grains, legumes, processed foods, and other agricultural products. All products must meet our quality standards and food safety requirements.

How do you ensure product quality?

We have strict quality control measures including farm inspections, product sampling, customer feedback systems, and quality ratings. Our team provides training on proper harvesting, handling, and packaging to maintain high standards.

Can I get help with modern farming techniques?

Absolutely! We provide access to smart farming technology, training programs, agricultural experts, and resources to help you improve your farming practices, increase yields, and reduce costs. This includes irrigation systems, crop monitoring, and precision farming tools.
