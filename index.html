Our Farming Story

At Farm Langu, we believe in sustainable farming practices that respect the land and produce the healthiest food possible. Our family has been farming this land for three generations, combining traditional wisdom with modern sustainable techniques.

We grow a variety of seasonal crops, raise free-range livestock, and maintain a healthy ecosystem on our farm.

Seasonal Growing Calendar

See what's growing and harvesting throughout the year

Spring (Mar-May)

  • Planting: Tomatoes, Peppers, Cucumbers
  • Harvesting: Spring Greens, Radishes, Asparagus

Summer (Jun-Aug)

  • Planting: Pumpkins, Squash, Melons
  • Harvesting: Tomatoes, Berries, Zucchini, Corn

Fall (Sep-Nov)

  • Planting: Garlic, Cover Crops, Winter Greens
  • Harvesting: Apples, Pumpkins, Root Vegetables

Winter (Dec-Feb)

  • Planting: Planning next season, Greenhouse starts
  • Harvesting: Winter Squash, Stored Crops, Greenhouse Greens
